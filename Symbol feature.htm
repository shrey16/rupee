FEATURES OF THE SYMBOL

The new sign is a combination of the Devanagari letter "?" (ra) and the Latin capital letter "R" without its vertical bar (similar to the R rotunda). The parallel lines at the top (with white space between them) are said to make an allusion to the tricolor Indian flag and also depict an equality sign which symbolizes the nation's desire to reduce economic disparity.

The final selected symbol was designed by D Udaya Kumar, a Ph D student at the Industrial Design Centre, IIT Bombay.
